A field series

R9700 + vLLM: getting real agentic AI work out of a mainstream professional PCIe card.

Twelve parts (so far) on bringing up an AMD Radeon AI PRO R9700 alongside a Gen 4 EPYC host, picking a runtime, and running a multi-agent workload end‑to‑end — the same card serving ~50 tok/s for one user scales to ~450–480 tok/s aggregate under 23–24 concurrent agents.

AMD Field AI Enthusiast · by Pradeep Nallimelli · June 2026
